Media Studies 200
Surveys the cultural and material roles media play around the world in the 21st century. Emphasis on building a common vocabulary for studying media as well as understanding how audio, images, video, and text mediate people's relation to culture.

MDIA 350 in Spring 2024: "This course traces the long history of the book from the manuscript codex to the eBook. We will explore the development of writing surfaces, inks, book formats, movable type, mechanization, and new media. We will discuss the various agents and institutions that make, censor, distribute, destroy, read, edit, write in, remediate, and collect books."
